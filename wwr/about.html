Mountain Rapids Rafting

Our mission

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and unforgettable white water rafting adventures. We believe outdoor adventures bring people together, build confidence, and create memories that last a lifetime.

History

Mountain Rapids Rafting was founded by a small group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of rivers and adventure with others.

Since then, the company has helped families, friends, and organizations experience the excitement and beauty of white water rafting.
